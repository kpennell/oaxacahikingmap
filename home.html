About Oaxaqueando

Hello. This site was made by Kyle Pennell. I've lived in Oaxaca since January 2014. I made Oaxaqueando because I thought having a map would make it easier to explore more cool things and places in Oaxaca.

If you have any suggestions or comments on things that need to be added or changed on here, please email me:


I will continue adding to this and improving the interface so that you and many other people can explore more of Oaxaca.

Thanks to Hoofing it in Oaxaca and Volunteer Oaxaca for letting me use some of their information.
